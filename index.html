About Panda Express

Panda Express has a great variety of Chinese cuisine. We serve a lot of different types of food. We serve lots of different kinds of chicken such as: kung pao, orange chicken, honey sesame chicken, and so much more. There are lots different types of rice and vegetables. We serve fried rice, boiled rice, white rice, and brown rice.
